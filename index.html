Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Tom

I started The Golf Marketing because golf clubs with incredible courses simply weren't using social media to spread awareness and showcase what they have — in its true light. Quite literally. The courses are stunning. Nobody was seeing them.

Most marketing agencies don't understand golf. They treat your club like a coffee shop or a gym — generic content, generic strategy, generic results. I built something different.

We don't take on non-golf clients. Every strategy, every piece of content, every system we build is tailored specifically to the venue in front of us — because no two clubs are the same, and your marketing shouldn't be either.
